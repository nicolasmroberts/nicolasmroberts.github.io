Welcome

I am currently a PhD student in the Structure and Tectonics group at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I work with Basil Tikoff. My research interests include early Earth crustal processes, microstructural shear zone kinematics, pluton emplacement, and statistical analysis of structural geology data. For my PhD, I am applying all these interests to investigate the kinematic and emplacement history of several Paleoarchean granitic complexes of the 3.5-3.2 Ga East Pilbara craton, Western Australia.
